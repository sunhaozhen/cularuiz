INSIGHTFUL VISION

CULARUIZ was founded by extraordinary entrepreneur Terry LaCore, along with close friends and allies Curtis Broome and Lisa Grossmann. Terry has founded several highly successful businesses that generated hundreds of millions in revenue. With insightful vision, he secured for CULARUIZ exclusive access to extraordinary, scientifically-advanced products in the renowned Stemology skincare line, as well as commissioning his development team to create the Souléver and BodiBalance product lines. Terry, Curtis and Lisa assembled a world-class team of respected colleagues and close friends, all of whom are the best at what they do, and have extensive experience throughout Asia.

EXPERIENCE AND EXPERTISE

Through both traditional business and network marketing, CULARUIZ’s core corporate leadership and management teams bring more than 150 combined years of experience to CULARUIZ. Each are renowned and respected in their various areas of expertise, and many have been sought-after consultants and confidantes to companies around the world. All have come together to help make the vision for CULARUIZ become a reality.

At Generation Now, we are devoted to creating a truly unique company for today’s economy and culture throughout Asia. Our corporate team is committed to providing dedicated women and men with the tools they need to realize their long-term financial goals.

TERRY LACORE
Founder and Chief Executive Officer

As a 20-year veteran of network marketing, Terry is a highly successful and renowned entrepreneur. He instills positivity and inspires belief everywhere he goes. With remarkable vision to see the possibilities, Terry’s business acumen paired with his extraordinary ability as a team builder has proven to be a prosperous combination for hundreds of thousands around the world. Known for his integrity, candor and innovation, he is respected as a business adviser and confidante throughout the industry. A devoted family man, Terry loves to spend time with his wife Jennifer and 4 children at their home in Texas, attending sporting events, and traveling. The LaCore family is active in school, community and charitable organizations.

CURTIS BROOME
Co-Founder and Worldwide President

Curtis brings to CULARUIZ a wealth of experience and knowledge in both traditional business and network marketing. Greatly successful in private business, as a public speaker, and as a corporate consultant, Curtis has 25 years of experience on both sides of the network marketing industry – as both a multi-million-dollar earner and a skilled corporate executive. His expertise as a communicator coupled with an acute business acumen set the foundation for his success as a trusted corporate executive.

As a former and current resident of Hong Kong, Curtis enjoys living in Greater China and experiencing its culture and heritage.

LISA GROSSMANN
Co-Founder and Chief Imagination Officer

With an incredible ability for inventive thinking and innovative vision, Lisa is an extraordinary businesswoman with acute insight. She’s become one of the most sought-after industry trainers and speakers, is considered a thought leader’s thought leader, and is a multi-million-dollar team builder in network marketing. Lisa is inspirational and generous with her time and talents. She readily shares her knowledge and wisdom, masterminds solutions, and helps others design and achieve their own successes.

Lisa enjoys music, traveling, and spending time with her husband and daughters.

JENIFER GRACE
General Counsel and Chief Compliance Officer

Jenifer brings to CULARUIZ her extensive legal and business expertise, managing both the legal and compliance departments. Jenifer is a trusted confidante of CEO Terry LaCore and an integral member of CULARUIZ’s innermost group of decision makers. Her energetic, no-nonsense style comes from many years representing the direct sales and nutritional health industries.

Jenifer earned a bachelor of arts degree in psychology from the University of Nevada, Reno, and a Juris Doctor from Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas. Jenifer is active in her community serving on several non-profit boards and participating with her running team in monthly social runs.

JOE WOOD
Chief Operations Officer, Worldwide

Joe brings to CULARUIZ his skillful management of daily global operations gained through many years in both traditional business and direct marketing. With extensive business experience in the Asian market, he serves as a highly respected and trusted colleague to CULARUIZ’s top executives and management teams. His effective allocation of operational resources is unmatched as he oversees the worldwide operations of CULARUIZ.

Joe holds an M.A. in Korean Literacy and enjoyed living internationally. He now loves spending time with his wife and four children at their home in Texas.

ANN ERLANDSSON
Chief Financial Officer

After receiving her Ph.D. in Business Studies, Ann continued her accounting career with a CPA firm in Los Angeles, CA. She has more than 15 years of accounting experience in a wide variety of industries and more than 5 years of experience as a Certified Public Accountant. She is proficient in e-commerce, international and network marketing accounting.

With her two boys, Ann loves to be active. She and her boys are involved in several non-competitive sports. As an avid runner, Ann started participating in triathlons last year. Fairly new to Texas, Ann, her husband and their boys enjoy exploring the area and going to movies.
