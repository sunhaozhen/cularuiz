Directions For Use

On clean, dry skin in an upward and outward direction, apply a thin layer (2ml) evenly on the face and neck. Remain moderately emotionless for 2-4 minutes while Fantastique dries. To rinse, splash off the top layer with cool clean water (do not rub) and gently pat dry. Follow with a water-based moisturizer and your normal skincare routine.

Caution: Pure biological agents, without any chemical additions, need to be frozen.

Chronodyn™
Fantastique contains the cell energizer Chronodyn™, which helps stimulate cell metabolism and strengthen the skin’s ability to regenerate. Chronodyn™ helps the skin recover its firmness and tone. In clinical studies, Chronodyn™ increased the skin’s firmness, tone and vitality and strengthened the skin’s resistance to fatigue and aging.

Essenskin™
Along with instantly taking away years from your appearance, Souléver Facial Firmer also provides long-term anti-aging benefits with Essenskin™, designed to boost cell metabolism and protein synthesis. Jojoba Esters
Jojoba is the only plant known to produce wax esters similar to those in our skin and is readily absorbed into the skin.
